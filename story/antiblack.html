what does anti-black mean?

The personal, cultural, social, legal, and structural attacks on black people. A term that some feel should replace racism as it is more focused on black people alone rather than all people of color.



who can be anti-black?

Anyone and everyone. Even black people. Internalized anti-blackness is a real thing that happens within black people. It has a lot to do with the context they grew up in and the media's portrayal of black people over the years.
